## What is HelixDesk AI?

**HelixDesk AI** is an intelligent IT support automation system that uses artificial intelligence and multiple AI agents working together to automatically resolve IT tickets—just like a team of IT support specialists working in harmony.

### Simple Explanation

Imagine you have a problem with your computer at work—maybe your email won't open, or the WiFi isn't working. Instead of:

- :hourglass: Waiting hours for IT support to respond
- :memo: Filling out long support forms
- :speaking_head: Explaining the same problem multiple times

You just type what's wrong in plain English, and **within seconds**, an AI team:

1. :brain: **Understands** your problem
2. :mag: **Searches** through thousands of solutions
3. :gift: **Gives you** the exact fix you need
4. :arrow_up: **Escalates to human IT** if it can't help

## Key Features

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-brain: **Natural Language Understanding**

    ---

    Type problems in plain English—no technical jargon required. The system understands context and intent.

-   :material-robot: **Multi-Agent Collaboration**

    ---

    Specialized AI agents (Classifier, Knowledge Base, Notifier) work together to resolve issues efficiently.

-   :material-vector-polygon: **Semantic Vector Search**

    ---

    Powered by Azure AI Search with 1536-dimensional embeddings for accurate solution matching.

-   :material-email-fast: **Automatic Escalation**

    ---

    If AI can't help, human IT is notified automatically with detailed ticket information.

-   :material-clock-fast: **Instant Response**

    ---

    Average response time: **2-5 seconds** (vs. 2-5 days traditional IT support).

-   :material-chart-line: **70-85% Resolution Rate**

    ---

    Automatically resolves routine IT issues, freeing IT teams for complex problems.
