# End User Guide

Welcome! This guide will help you use HelixDesk AI to resolve your IT issues quickly.

## How to Submit a Ticket

1. **Open the HelixDesk AI Application**
   - Navigate to the application URL provided by your IT department
   - You'll see a simple chat interface

2. **Describe Your Problem**
   - Type your issue in plain English
   - Be as specific as possible
   - Examples:
     - "I can't access my email"
     - "WiFi is not connecting on my laptop"
     - "Printer is showing offline"

3. **Get Your Solution**
   - The AI will analyze your problem
   - Within 5-10 seconds, you'll receive:
     - Step-by-step solution
     - Relevant screenshots or links
     - Escalation to human IT if needed

## What HelixDesk AI Can Help With

- **Password Issues**: Reset instructions, account lockouts
- **Email Problems**: Access issues, configuration help
- **Network Issues**: WiFi, VPN, connectivity
- **Software Issues**: Installation, updates, licenses
- **Hardware Issues**: Printer, monitor, keyboard problems
- **Account Access**: Permissions, system access requests

## Example Interactions

### Example 1: Email Access

**You**: "I can't log into my email"

**HelixDesk AI**:
> I'll help you resolve your email access issue. Here are the steps:
>
> 1. Try resetting your password at portal.office.com
> 2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
> 3. Try accessing email in incognito mode
>
> If this doesn't work, I'm escalating your ticket to IT support.

### Example 2: WiFi Connection

**You**: "My laptop won't connect to the office WiFi"

**HelixDesk AI**:
> Let's troubleshoot your WiFi connection:
>
> 1. Forget the network and reconnect
> 2. Check if WiFi is enabled
> 3. Restart your laptop
>
> Here's a detailed guide: [WiFi Troubleshooting Steps]

## Tips for Better Results

- **Be Specific**: Instead of "computer broken", say "computer won't turn on"
- **Include Error Messages**: Copy any error text you see
- **Mention What You've Tried**: "I already restarted but still having issues"
- **Provide Context**: "This started after the Windows update"

## What Happens Next

### If AI Resolves Your Issue
- Mark the solution as helpful
- The ticket is automatically closed
- No further action needed!

### If AI Cannot Help
- Your ticket is automatically escalated to human IT support
- An email is sent to the IT team with your issue details
- You'll receive a follow-up within 24 hours
